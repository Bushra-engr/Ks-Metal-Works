Our Story

K'S Metal Works was established with a clear vision: to provide premium quality metal fabrication solutions for the digital display industry. Based in New Delhi, we have grown to become a trusted name for Digital Standees and Video Wall Enclosures.

We take pride in our in-house manufacturing—from laser cutting to powder coating—ensuring complete quality control. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has cemented our position as a preferred partner in India.
